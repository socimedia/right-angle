Respect

The earth shattering ‘Aretha’ experience presented by Australia’s own ‘Soul Mama’ Angie Narayan combining storytelling, song and soul!

The delightfully heart warming, humorous and uplifting show, R•E•S•P•E•C•T journeys through Aretha Franklin’s courageous life of love, tragedy and triumph!

Showcasing her greatest hits over the last 50 years, R•E•S•P•E•C•T is a masterful piece of storytelling, exploring the Diva’s life with the help of her siblings, through her unique childhood, church, relationships and the turbulent music industry!
