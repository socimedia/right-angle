Game of Thrones Musical

Winter is coming, literally, as “Game of Thrones” is told through 90 minutes of hilarity! MUSICAL THRONES:

A PARODY of Ice and Fire, a sending-up of the popular HBO drama, “Game of Thrones" is in development for a 2019-2020 national tour.

Fans of the most-talked-about show on television can now get their fantasy fix live-on-stage with this hilarious parody. Composed by the hysterical madmen behind the long-running Silence! The Musical, Jon and Al Kaplan, Musical Thrones: A Parody of Fire and Ice brings your most beloved and be-hated characters to life as you journey through the first seven seasons of the Emmy® Award-winning “Game of Thrones” series. Sing and dance along with Daenerys and her dragons, Joffery and the jolly members of the Lannister and Stark families in this love letter to fans.

Guests will be transported to “Thrones”’s magical locations (if they close their eyes) where bloodthirsty musical theater comics leave no joke unturned in serving up the show’s notorious violence, power struggles, manipulation and sex – plus, worst yet, a ballad or two. The show is preparing for battle, so saddle up and come down to King’s Landing, and hold onto your swords!
